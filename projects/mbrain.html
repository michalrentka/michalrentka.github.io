M-Brain

I have developed 2 iOS applications for M-Brain. These applications are built based on their services M-Adaptive and Plaza. Only M-Brain customers have access to these applications.

M-Adaptive

M-Brain’s M-Adaptive app allows you to access content from the global media listening software of the same name. M-Adaptive’s search engine is capable of crawling media sources in 68 languages using optimized language detection. With M-Adaptive you can monitor and analyze both editorial as well as social media content.

Plaza

M-Brain’s Intelligence Plaza app allows you to access content from the standard Intelligence Plaza, the world’s leading market intelligence platform used by over 50,000 corporate decision makers around the globe. The Intelligence Plaza brings together external competitor insights, with internal insights from colleagues within the organization.
